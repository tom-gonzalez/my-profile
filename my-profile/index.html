Tom Gonzalez

My name is Tom Gonzalez and since September 2020 I have been a student in the first year of the Bachelor's programme at the prestigious ESSEC in Paris. Before I started graduate studies, I was involved in citizenship and political action, adventures that allowed me to nurture and cultivate a certain intellectual thirst to discover and learn about many different subjects. Through a two-year mandate as a youth representative in my region, I developed management and communication skills, and I became aware of the importance of research and adventure in order to develop myself as an individual. Beyond my involvement in citizen debate and action, I like to sharpen my sensitivity. For nearly three years now, I have been learning to play the piano on my own. It is now an instrument that I play at a very high level. The piano allows me to escape and develop new connections. Since I was very young, I have also had the good fortune to be able to travel abroad often and thus to maintain a relationship of complicity with the unknown which, instead of frightening me, comforts me. It is in an international, enterprising, intense and dynamic environment that I need to be in order to bring my hyperactivity to life and thus animate the untapped potential within me.
